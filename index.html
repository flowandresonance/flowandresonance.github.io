Flow & Resonance

When we allow ourselves to move naturally and feel deeply, something profound emerges. A space where physical expression meets emotional understanding, and where authentic patterns of being can develop through the wisdom of both body and mind.

Our Vision

This practice integrates psychodynamic and somatic psychotherapy with embodied movement exploration to support the emergence of authentic self. Through flowing with what naturally resonates - whether in emotional expression or physical movement - we create space for hidden aspects to emerge, be felt, acknowledged, and integrated.

Movement for Everyone

Our approach adapts to your unique needs, whether you're just beginning your movement journey, managing an injury, or exploring new possibilities in your established practice. Every movement, no matter how subtle, can lead to increased capacity and awareness.

For Practitioners

We're building an inclusive community bringing together practitioners from diverse backgrounds to support authentic expression and freedom through movement and awareness. Our collaboration spans across:

  • Psychotherapy and somatic therapy
  • Movement and embodiment practices (yoga, dance, capoeira, martial arts)
  • Body awareness and meditation practices (breathwork, qigong, tai chi)
  • Athletic and acrobatic disciplines (gymnastics, acrobatics, parkour)
  • Expressive and performing arts
  • Other somatic and movement traditions

If you're interested in our integrated approach to supporting authentic expression and embodied freedom, we'd love to connect with you.

Our vision is to create a space where diverse modalities come together to support true freedom - the freedom to feel all feelings, to embody all parts of ourselves, and to move in ways that authentically express who we are. When we create safe spaces for this exploration, we invite both practitioners and clients to discover new dimensions of embodied awareness and self-expression.
